Profile

Where Moments Become Masterpieces

I'm not just a photographer or travel guide—I'm your local friend who has lived across four countries on three continents. From the vibrant rhythm of Rio de Janeiro to the ancient temples of Indonesia, from the cultural tapestry of Mexico to the innovation hubs of the United States, I've not just visited these places—I've belonged to them.

This multicultural lens fuels everything at Corbellini Horizons. Whether I'm capturing sonic energy at a live show, guiding you through hidden neighborhoods, or filming cinematic 8K footage from the sky, I bring the perspective of someone who understands what it means to truly belong.
